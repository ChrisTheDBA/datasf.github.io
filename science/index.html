What is DataScienceSF?

DataScienceSF is a way for you to harness the power of advanced analytics and applied statistics for the challenges you face in your department.

This new service from DataSF aims to help departments achieve more with their existing resources and processes.  

Through a 4 month engagement, DataSF’s Data Science team and your department will refine a problem, identify statistical methods to address it, and develop and institute a service change to improve your work. Projects are chosen through a bi-annual selection process.  The final product of DataScienceSF isn't a recommendation or a report but a service change.

What are the expectations for the department?

First and foremost we are committed to realizable service change.  

To do this we work closely with a Department Champion.  The Department Champion is our liaison within the department who helps refine the problem statement and identify paths to service change.

Engagements last between 1-4 months.  The engagement will be an iterative process as we refine the original problem statement into something answerable with the data available and implementable within any service change constraints.  

As part of the engagement we may schedule 'ride-alongs' where members of the data science team shadow employees to identify relevant implementation factors that should be incorporated into the statistical model.

We estimate the Departmental Champion will, on average, need to dedicate 5 hours a week to this project.
