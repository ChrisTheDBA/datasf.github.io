DataScienceSF

What is DataScienceSF?

DataScienceSF is a way for you to harness the power of advanced analytics and applied statistics for the challenges you face in your department.

This new service from DataSF aims to help departments achieve more with their existing resources and processes.

Through a 4 month engagement, DataSF’s Data Science team and your department will refine a problem, identify statistical methods to address it, and develop and institute a service change to improve your work. The final product of DataScienceSF isn't a recommendation or a report but service change.
